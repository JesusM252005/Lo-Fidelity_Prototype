About

The Smithsonian Institution is the world's largest museum, education, and research complex. We are a community of learning and an opener of doors. On May 8th through June 8th, join us on a voyage of discovery and explore our newest gallery located in the Smithsonian Arts & Industries Building. Come witness a dropped collection from the past and enjoy the pleasance of shiny and chrome diecast cars.
